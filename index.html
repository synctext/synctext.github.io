Technology

We developed technology to promote social change concerning institutions, companies, infrastructures and resources. Our technology tries to alter the essence of capitalism. Our micro-economy of robots offers benefits to everybody and is democratically ruled, not owned by any elite. Our technology is designed to withstand all known government attacks, lawyer-based attacks, and political interest groups. Our technology is based on smartphones which can transmit money and information freely and are under democratic oversight.

We introduce a DAO (Decentralized Autonomour Organization). In the DAO, there is no central governing body. In fact, all those taking part in the DAO decide on what and how. In a DAO, the rules are transparent and written by the shareholders. Blockchain technology is the critical backbone of a DAO, and ours is no exception.
