About me

Over the last few years I've enjoyed many opportunities to explore the fantastic field of Computer Science, both here in Delft as well as at the University of Sydney. On this page you'll find exactly what I've learned and what I enjoy.

Experience

Through my curriculum I've experienced and enjoyed different sides of the cyber security field, ranging from cryptography to management. Some courses offer a true deep-dive in the technology, Advanced Network Security is a prime example of this. Furthermore, I've been selected as a Tech Ambassador by the ASML scholarship programme, where we focus on the skills that you do not learn through education.
